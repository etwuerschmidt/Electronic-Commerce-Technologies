The Problem

Currently, most of your information that is sent through servers is readable by anyone with access to that server or who can tap into the transmitted packets. All data is stored in its original, unaltered state, and is often sold to businesses who use that personal information to track browsing habits without your knowledge. In an age where everyone is concerned about their privacy, why are you letting your data be accessible to anyone?

What We Do

  • Refuse the sale of any encrypted data to outside companies
